This dataset contains survey responses collected from Dutch participants as part of an empirical study on phishing susceptibility. The dataset includes demographic information of participants alongside their responses to a set of phishing and legitimate emails, designed to measure susceptibility across different persuasion-based email characteristics, including authority, urgency, and threat cues. The data were collected to examine how demographic and persuasion factors influence phishing vulnerability among the Dutch population and form part of a broader cross-cultural investigation comparing phishing susceptibility patterns across different national contexts.
